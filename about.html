About Chris Courage

Biography

My name is Chris Courage, a PhD candidate at Arts University Bournemouth in the Creative Technologies department. I earned my MA (Distinction) in Computational Arts at Goldsmiths University London in 2022, and my BA in Graphic Design from AUB in 2020.

Research Interests

  • Human Computer Interaction
  • Tangible and Embodied Interaction
  • Data Materialisations
  • Internet of Things
  • Ubiquitous Computing

Teaching

I am a visiting tutor at Arts University Bournemouth, where I deliver creative computing workshops, website design, and sensing/data moshing workshops for the Graphic Design department.
